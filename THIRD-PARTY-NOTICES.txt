Why the distribution is under the GPL
-------------------------------------
RabbitEars bundles the VLC media-player plugin set, which includes modules
licensed under the GNU GPL (v2 or later). Because GPL-licensed components are
included in what is distributed, the RabbitEars binary distribution as a whole
is conveyed under the GNU GPL (see LICENSE). The libVLC core itself is LGPL and
is dynamically linked and shipped as a separate, replaceable library.

complete corresponding source code is published by VideoLAN and can be obtained

This satisfies the requirement to provide access to the corresponding source
